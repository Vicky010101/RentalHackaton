Guidelines and Policies

  • All customer information will be kept confidential and used only for processing and delivering orders.
  • Customers are advised to handle the rented items with care and store them in clean, dry environments.
  • Customers should notify the business immediately if they encounter any issues with the delivered product.
  • Inspect items upon delivery and report any damages or discrepancies within 24 hours.
  • Customers should not clean or alter the rented outfits. Professional cleaning is included in the rental fee.
  • The security deposit will be refunded within 7 working days after the item is returned in acceptable condition.
  • Damage assessment will be conducted by the business, and charges will be deducted accordingly.
  • Late return fees will be calculated per day and communicated clearly at the time of rental.
  • Customers are encouraged to select the appropriate size carefully; the business holds no responsibility for fit issues unless size was incorrectly listed.
  • All customer information will be kept confidential and used only for processing and delivering orders.

Terms and Conditions

  • An invoice copy will be attached with every order for transparency.
  • The maximum rental period is 5 days.
  • Orders must be placed at least 3 days prior to the desired rental date.
  • Orders will be delivered 1 day before the rental start date.
  • Both the seller and receiver must provide valid authentication documents (e.g., Aadhaar/PAN card and permanent address proof).
  • A security deposit equivalent to 30% of the item's original price will be collected and refunded upon the item's safe return.
  • If damage occurs, the repair/replacement cost will be deducted from the security deposit.
  • Late returns will incur additional fees as per the delay duration.
